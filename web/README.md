**Your Year in Music, Reimagined.**

Playlist Wrapped is an interactive timeline and statistics dashboard for your Spotify playlists. It allows you to visualize how your playlist has evolved over time, track song rankings, and discover hidden trends in your listening habits.

## Features

### üìÖ Interactive Timeline
- **Daily History**: Scroll through your playlist's history day by day to see exactly what changed.
- **Auto-Scroll**: The page automatically focuses on the Day View when you interact with the timeline.
- **Playback Controls**: Play, pause, and seek through time to watch your playlist evolve.

### üìä Deep Statistics
- **Longest Streak**: Discover which tracks stayed on your playlist for the longest consecutive run.
- **Best Average Rank**: See which songs consistently performed the best.
- **One Hit Wonders**: Identify tracks that appeared for only a single day.
- **Unique #1s**: A hall of fame for every track that reached the top spot.
- **Jump to History**: Click on any track in the stats to instantly jump to the day it performed best.

### ‚å®Ô∏è Keyboard Navigation
- **Arrow Keys**: Use `Left` and `Right` arrow keys to navigate the timeline day by day.
- **Spacebar**: Press `Space` to toggle playback.

## Tech Stack

- **Framework**: [Next.js 15](https://nextjs.org/) (App Router)
- **Styling**: [Tailwind CSS](https://tailwindcss.com/)
- **Animations**: [Framer Motion](https://www.framer.com/motion/) & Custom CSS
- **Font**: [Geist](https://vercel.com/font)
